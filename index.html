DIGITAL LOGIC

I believe that mastering Digital Logic increased my knowledge of electronic topics. All microprocessor-based systems, such as those in computers, robotics, cars, and industrial control systems, are built on digital electronics. It gives me a general introduction to digital electronics and covers a lot of significant ideas, parts, and instruments. I received comprehensive education on digital principles, ranging from fundamental ideas to programmable logic devices. Additionally, I had the opportunity to attempt something new by conducting laboratory experiments using the simulator software and real devices and circuits that were covered in class.

DISCRETE STRUCTURE

I am convinced that studying discrete structure has exposed me to the ideas behind and practical uses of the concept in computer science. Set theory, proving strategies, relations, functions, recurrence relations, counting techniques, graph theory, trees, and finite automata are among the subjects studied in this course. By the end, I knew how to model electronic devices and issues using deterministic finite automata finite state machines, analyse and solve problems using recurrence relations and counting techniques, apply graph theory and trees to real-world problems, and use set theory, relations, and functions to solve computer science problems.
